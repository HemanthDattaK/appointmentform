Help

How To Join The Meeting:(PC Users)

  • Click on the join meeting in home page navigation bar.
  • Enter your phone number given in the appointment form.
  • Click on submit.
  • Enter your Name and click Join Meeting.
  • A pop-up appear and select the option Wait For Moderator.
  • When the moderator accepts your request you will be join in the meeting.

How To Join The Meeting:(Mobile Users)

  • Click on the join meeting in home page navigation bar.
  • Enter your phone number given in the appointment form.
  • Click on submit.
  • Click on Join in Browser
  • Enter your Name and click Join Meeting.
  • A pop-up appear and select the option Wait For Moderator.
  • When the moderator accepts your request you will be join in the meeting.
